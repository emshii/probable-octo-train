Summer Holiday

Siargao Beach

Siargao Beach in the Philippines has been famous for their Cloud Nine waves. It is because for their iconic tube-like appearance, giving hard-core surfers the ultimate challenge. But Siargao Island is not only for surfers but also for non-surfers who wants to relax and swim.

Sevilla's Hanging Bridge

It is located on the Island of Bohol, a bridge made entirely of bamboo swings almost 40 meters in the air over the Loboc River. This is perfect for people who loves adventure, the hanging bridge walk will be a vacation experience you will never forget.

Pagsanjan Falls

Pagsanjan falls is located in Laguna, Philippines. Pagsanjan falls is perfect for adventoruous people because not only you will admire the waterfalls but also ride a dugout canoe to take you upstream hrough tropical landscapes, where you will be able to swim in natural pools and pass through caves.

Manila Ocean Park

Summer vacation is coming and Manila Ocean Park is a must-see, with its interactive marine life with the Sharks and Rays Encounter,Trails to Antarctica, or the fish spa where fish clean your feet in a small pool of water. It is perfect for family outings.

Shipwreck Diving

One of the Best thing to do in a Summer Vacation is going to a beach and swim. But diving shipwrecks are beyond epic. The sunken ships to explore on Sangat Island in Coron are some of the best diving sites in the world. The wrecks are at varied depths that are suitable for both deep water scuba divers and shallow water snorkeling. The shipwrecks, many from WWII, have become habitats for diverse fish species. The clear water makes for easy viewing of the shipwrecks and underwater wildlife.

Mayon Volcano

Summer is coming and visiting Mayon Volcano in Albay province on Luzon Island is a must-see in the Philippines. It's an active stratovolcano with an elevation of more than 2,400 meters. The Mayon volcano has a perfect cone peak.
